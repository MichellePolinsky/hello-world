Powerlifting

Powerlifting is the best sport, in my opinion. Anyone can do it. To begin powerlifting you need the following

  • Gym
  • Shoes*
  • Belt
  • Wrist Wraps

*Different shoes are recommended for each lift. Squat shoes should be of firm bottom and a lift in the heel. For deadlift you will want a flat thin sole. Bench shoes can be whatever feels most comfortable to you. Some people prefer a regular sneaker and some like to wear their squat shoes

One of the greatest Powerlifters of our time is Jennifer Thompson.
