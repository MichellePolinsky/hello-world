Powerlifting is a great sport that anyone can do. To begin powerlifting you need the following:

  • Gym with free weights
  • Shoes*
  • Belt
  • Wrist Wraps
  • Dedication

*Different shoes are recommended for each lift. Squat shoes should be of firm bottom and a lift in the heel. For deadlift you will want a flat thin sole shoe such as converse or a deadlift slipper. Bench shoes can be whatever feels most comfortable to you. Some people prefer a regular sneaker and some like to wear their squat shoes.
